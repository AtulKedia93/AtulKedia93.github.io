Co-hosted the MidWest Relativity Meeting on 22-24 October, 2020

The Center for Astrophysics at Notre Dame University (CANDU) hosted the 30th Mid-West Relativity (Virtual) Meeting on October 22-24, 2020. The meeting was attended by more than 150 relativists located around the world, delivering over 80 talks in topics covering different research topics in General Relativity. Meeting website.
